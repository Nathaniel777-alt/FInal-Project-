My Hobbies

Gundams • Anime Figurines • Manhwas

Gundams (Gunpla)

Building Gundam model kits, also known as Gunpla, is one of my favorite hobbies. I enjoy carefully assembling each piece, applying decals, and posing the finished model. Every Gundam design has its own personality, from sleek high-speed units to heavily armored mobile suits.

  • Grades I enjoy: HG, MG, and RG
  • Favorite activities: building, panel lining, and posing
  • Why I like it: relaxing, creative, and rewarding

Anime Figurines

Collecting anime figurines allows me to celebrate my favorite characters and series. Each figure captures a moment, pose, or emotion that makes the character memorable. I enjoy organizing and displaying them in a clean, aesthetic setup.

  • Types: scale figures, prize figures, and action figures
  • Focus: quality sculpting and accurate details
  • Display style: minimal, clean, and well-lit

Manhwa

Reading manhwa is another hobby I really enjoy. The artwork, storytelling, and unique pacing make it different from manga and anime. I especially like stories with strong world-building and character development.

  • Genres I enjoy: action, fantasy, and romance
  • Why I read manhwa: engaging stories and beautiful art
  • Favorite format: digital reading
